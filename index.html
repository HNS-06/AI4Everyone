Why Kerala Needs AI Literacy Now

Addressing the challenges and opportunities of AI in our society

Literacy for All & Global Model

Equitable AI education that empowers every citizen to understand and apply AI responsibly. Kerala can position itself as a global model for AI education rooted in social responsibility.

Creators, Not Just Consumers

Enabling Keralites to innovate and build AI-driven solutions, not just use them. We're fostering a culture of creation and innovation.

Knowledge as a Public Good

Making AI knowledge accessible to all with open, grassroots-first, and equitable approaches where everyone becomes both learner and teacher.

Reflection: What I Learned About AI

Building this website with AI tools demonstrated how AI can accelerate the creative process while still requiring human direction and curation. The collaboration between human creativity and AI capabilities resulted in a more polished and innovative final product than either could achieve alone. This aligns perfectly with TinkerHub's mission to show practical, creative AI usage. I learned that AI is not a replacement for human creativity but a powerful amplifier that, when guided by human intention, can produce remarkable results.
